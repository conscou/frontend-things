about me


Greetings!

I'm an actor, singer, educator, administrator, aspiring activist, and more--- I'm Chris Price. I hope to leave my mark on the world as an artist and teacher. Originally from Southern New Jersey (right outside of Philadelphia), I grew up acting in school productions, singing at church, and discovering my addiction to the performing arts. Thanks to God, my family, and friends, I have been able to pursue a more serious track with performing in New York City and Northern New Jersey, all while still satisfying this thirst for academia.

As an advocate for education, social change, and the arts, I also strive to bring together the worlds of the performing arts and social justice in order to inspire others to contribute to society. I have interned with NYC's Broadway Bound Kids and recently acted as the Education and Engagement intern at The Broadway League. I currently work at the American Theatre Wing in the Program Department. Additionally, I have trained in acting, voice, directing, and movement, and love collaborating with others as both a performer and creator. I received my Master of Arts degree in Educational Theatre from New York University in New York City and my Bachelor of Arts degree in English (Feminist Studies and Literature concentration) with a minor in music from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ.

I love food (lots of it), puppies, and exploring the world.

Noblesse oblige.

-Chris
