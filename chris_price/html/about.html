about.


Chris Price aspires to leave his mark on the world as an artist and educator. Originally from Southern New Jersey (right outside of Philadelphia), Chris grew up acting in school productions, singing at church, and discovering his addiction to the performing arts. With the support of his family and friends, he has been able to pursue a more serious track with performing in New York City and Northern New Jersey, all while still satisfying this thirst for academia.

As an advocate for education, social change, and the arts, Chris also strives to bring together the worlds of the performing arts and social justice in order to inspire others to contribute to society. Chris has interned with NYC's Broadway Bound Kids and recently acted as the Education and Engagement intern at The Broadway League. Chris received his Bachelor of Arts degree in English (Feminist Studies and Literature concentration) from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ. He currently attends New York University in New York City, where he is earning his Master's of Arts degree in Educational Theatre.
